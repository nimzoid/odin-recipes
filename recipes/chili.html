Vegetarian Chili con Carne and Rice

This is a recipe for a vegetarian chili con carne served with rice.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • half a medium-sized onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 chilli
  • paprika
  • cumin
  • dried oregano
  • tin of chopped tomatoes
  • vegetarian mince
  • fresh coriander
  • tin of kidney beans
  • salt
  • long grain rice

Steps

  1. Heat some vegetable oil in a saucepan
  2. Add the onion, finely diced, and add the chopped garlic and chilli after a couple of minutes. Saute until the onions are translucent.
  3. Add the tomatoes and mince, a handful of chopped coriander and salt to taste, then simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. In another pan of boiling water, add the rice and boil for 15 minutes
  5. Add the tin of kidney beans to the chili.
  6. Drain the rice and top with the chili. Garnish with coriander and enjoy!
