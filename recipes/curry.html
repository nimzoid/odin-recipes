Vegetarian Chicken Curry and Rice

This is a recipe for a vegetarian chicken curry served with rice.

Ingredients

  • vegetable oil
  • one onion
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • thumb of ginger
  • curry powder
  • tin of chopped tomatoes
  • vegetarian chicken
  • fresh coriander
  • salt
  • basmati rice

Steps

  1. Heat some vegetable oil in a saucepan
  2. Add the onion, finely diced, and add the chopped garlic and ginger after a couple of minutes. Saute until the onions are translucent.
  3. Add a heaped tablespoon of curry powder and fry until fragrant.
  4. Add the tomatoes and chicken, a handful of chopped coriander and salt to taste, then simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. In another pan of boiling water, add the rice and boil for 15 minutes
  6. Drain the rice and top with the curry. Garnish with coriander and enjoy!
